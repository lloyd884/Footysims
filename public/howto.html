❓ Frequently Asked Questions

When does my data plan start?
Your plan starts when you first connect to a network in your destination country — not when you install the eSIM.
Can I keep my regular SIM in?
Yes! Your phone can have both your regular SIM and the FootySIMs eSIM active at the same time. Just set the eSIM as your data SIM when you arrive.
Can I reuse the eSIM?
No — each eSIM can only be installed once. Once your plan expires you'll need to purchase a new one.
I can't find my QR code
Log in to footysims.com, go to your dashboard, and tap "Show QR & Details" next to your plan. Your QR code is always available there.
