📱 How to Install Your eSIM

Follow these simple steps to get connected in minutes. No physical SIM needed!

1

Open your Camera app

Point your camera at the QR code shown on your FootySIMs dashboard.

2

Tap the notification

A banner will appear at the top of your screen saying "eSIM Activation". Tap it.

3

Tap "Continue" then "Add Mobile Plan"

Your iPhone will download the eSIM profile. This takes about 30 seconds.

4

Label your eSIM

Choose a label like "Travel" or "FootySIMs" to tell it apart from your main SIM.

5

Set Default Line

Keep your main SIM as default for calls/texts. Use FootySIMs eSIM for mobile data only.

💡 Go to Settings → Mobile Data → select FootySIMs eSIM for data
6

Enable Data Roaming

Go to Settings → Mobile Data → tap your FootySIMs eSIM → turn on Data Roaming.

✅ You're connected! Your eSIM activates when you land.
1

Open Settings

Go to Settings → Connections → SIM Manager (may vary by brand).

2

Add eSIM

Tap "Add mobile plan" or "Add eSIM" then choose "Scan QR code".

3

Scan the QR Code

Point your camera at the QR code on your FootySIMs dashboard.

4

Confirm and Download

Follow the prompts to download and activate your eSIM profile.

5

Set as Data SIM

In SIM Manager, set FootySIMs as your preferred data SIM.

💡 Keep your main SIM active for calls — only use FootySIMs for data abroad.
6

Enable Roaming

Make sure Data Roaming is enabled for your FootySIMs eSIM.

✅ You're all set! Your eSIM activates when you arrive at your destination.

FAQ

When does my eSIM start?
Your data plan starts the moment you first use data in the destination country — not when you install it.
Can I keep my normal number?
Yes! Your FootySIMs eSIM is data-only. Your normal SIM stays active for calls and texts.
Can I install it before I travel?
Yes — install it at home and it will activate automatically when you arrive at your destination.
What if I lose my QR code?
Log back into your FootySIMs dashboard — your QR code is always available there.
Does it work for calls?
FootySIMs eSIMs are data-only. Use WhatsApp, FaceTime or any internet calling app for free calls abroad.
