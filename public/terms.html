💰 Refund Policy

To request a refund, contact us within 24 hours of purchase with your order email and we will process it promptly.

📶 eSIM Terms of Use

Compatibility: eSIMs are only compatible with unlocked, eSIM-capable devices. It is your responsibility to check your device is compatible before purchasing. We cannot refund purchases made on incompatible devices.

Single use: Each eSIM QR code can only be scanned and installed once. Once installed, the QR code cannot be reused. Your eSIM details (SM-DP+ address and activation code) remain accessible in your FootySIMs account for manual re-installation if needed.

Data validity: Your data plan starts when you first use data in the destination country — not when you install the eSIM. Unused data at the end of the validity period is not refundable or transferable.

Network coverage: Coverage depends on local network availability. FootySIMs is not liable for network outages or poor signal in remote areas.
