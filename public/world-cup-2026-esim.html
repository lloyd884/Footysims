What Is a Travel eSIM?

An eSIM is a digital SIM card built into your phone. Instead of swapping physical SIM cards, you simply scan a QR code and your phone connects to a local network in seconds. No queuing at airport kiosks. No tiny SIM card ejector tools. No worrying about losing your UK SIM.
